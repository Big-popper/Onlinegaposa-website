INSTRUCTIONS

APPLICATION PROCEDURE FOR PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS

  1. Click on Fresh Application
  2. Complete the Basic Details Form (BDF)
  3. Click on "CONTINUE"
  4. If you are ready to pay, click on "PAY NOW" else click on "SAVE & EXIT"
  5. Complete your payment process
  6. Return to site(Online Application Portal), login and complete Detailed Application Form (DAF)
  7. Supply your Application Number as Username and your Surname as the password
  8. Supply all required information
  9. Enter your O'level result(s)
  10. Click on "SUBMIT" to save your completed information online
  11. Upload passport (configuration: JPG,120*120px and 20kb)
  12. Click on "Submit" to complete application process
  13. Print Detailed Application Form (DAF).
